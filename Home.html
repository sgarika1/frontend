Programs of Study

The department offers the undergraduate major of Computer Science. We also offer two master degree programs: Computer Science, and Computer Information Systems and Cyber Security.

Scholarships

Several undergraduate scholarships and graduate assistantships are available for students majoring in Computer Science, CIS, and Cyber Security.

Student clubs and organizations

We have three active student clubs: Mathematics Club, Computer Science Club, and Engineering Club.

Faculty and Staff

The Computer Science faculty and staff are available to help you succeed through phone, email, or office visits.

Academic Advising

Our advisors ensure you stay on track toward graduation and help you prepare for career opportunities.

Research interns and seminars

Our faculty conduct research in computing and mathematical sciences, providing opportunities for student involvement.
